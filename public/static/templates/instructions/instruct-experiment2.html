Instructions



You will see many mazes with different color goals and these colors will be repeated throughout the game. The color of a maze controls the rules of the maze, determining both which keys move you around in the maze and which goal is valuable.


You will have to learn the rules but for each color, the rules are always the same. For example, if you find that the button "j" moves the blue circle up, it will do so in every square and maze with a blue circle. Likewise, if you learn that the "A" goal is worth 10 points in a green maze, "A" will be worth 10 points in every other green maze you see.
